HRMS User Guide

This guide provides instructions on how to use the HRMS application.

Getting Started

To begin, you need to log in with the username and password provided by the HR department.

Dashboard

The dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It provides a quick overview of your status.

  • Check-In/Check-Out: You can mark your attendance for the day using the "Check In" and "Check Out" buttons.
  • Current Status: The dashboard displays your current check-in time for the day.

Attendance

The attendance page allows you to view attendance records.

  • Your Attendance: You can view your own attendance history.
  • All Employee Attendance (Admin, HR, Accountant): If you have Admin, HR, or Accountant roles, you can view the attendance records of all employees. You can also search for attendance by date and employee name, and export the data to a Google Sheet.

Leave Management

This section allows you to manage your leaves.

  • Apply for Leave: You can apply for leave by selecting the leave type, start date, and end date. The system will automatically calculate the number of working days, excluding holidays and non-working Saturdays.
  • Leave Balance: Your current Casual Leave (CL) balance is displayed. CLs are accrued at a rate of 1.5 days per month, prorated based on the day of the month.
  • Leave Approval: When you apply for leave, an email notification is sent to your manager for approval.

Profile Page

The Profile page shows your personal and employment details stored in the system.

  • User Details: You can view your information such as name, employee ID, job title, department, and joining date.
  • Salary Information: Please note that for privacy and security, your salary details are not displayed on this page.

Admin Features

These features are only available to users with the "Admin" role.

System Logs

View system-level logs and activities.

Accrue Monthly Leave

Manually trigger the process to accrue monthly leaves for all employees.

Apply Yearly Carry-over

Initiate the process to carry over remaining leaves from the previous year to the new year.

My Documents & HR Documents

These sections are for document management. They are currently under development.
